# Shell Startup Engine - Mastering Early-Stage Growth and Expanding Market Reach

Hear from the 8 groundbreaking energy and sustainability startups selected for the Shell StartUp Engine Singapore 2024/25 accelerator programme: 3Y Energy, Beecharge Innovation Group, Biotech Energy Limited, CRecTech, Factorem, FootprintIQ, MarinaChain, and OffsetFarm.

This session will also feature a presentation by Kauan von Novack (Global CEO of Startupbootcamp) on the current state of energy and climate technology and innovation.
