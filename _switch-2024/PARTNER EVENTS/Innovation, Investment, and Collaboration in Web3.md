WEB3WED are presenting a stream at SWITCH 2024 that focuses on innovation, investment, and collaboration in the Web3 space and will include one of this month's WEB3WED monthly meetup.

This 4-hour event will provide an engaging platform for thought leaders, startups, and key industry players to share insights, explore opportunities, and foster partnerships. The event will cater to a diverse audience of innovators, investors, and industry professionals, offering valuable content on the latest trends and future potential of blockchain technology.
