# K-Startup IR Demo Day in Singapore
The K-STARTUP IR Demoday is designed to bring together innovative startups and investors, offering a platform for 13 Korean startups to showcase their business ideas, products, and services to potential investors and partners. This event serves as a pivotal networking opportunity, fostering collaboration and investment in emerging technologies and services.

All startups will also participate in SWITCH 2024 (October 28-30, Marina Bay Sands Expo)!

Be sure to visit their booths to meet even more innovative Korean startups!
