#### **Where Innovation Happens at one-north**

![](https://lh7-us.googleusercontent.com/HMAXHSPVtV4U6Mysi8yJpAJSJ4mosvjonneSRdXBoBtNLGwjZBiWEzXNPNP6bSWA4Bm_sjFd-oDYG6mJKVksU32245DipJnJKeNmhHoB2WUw6VcfXvuW5PyrKrTCif0Urg54aoYNc8cHoSGUwicCP30)

SWITCH’s Signature Lab Crawls will be returning this October 2023 and will be curated alongside [SWITCH @ one-north!](https://www.switchsg.org/2023/programmes/at-one-north/)

JTC’s [one-north](https://www.jtc.gov.sg/find-land/land-for-long-term-development/one-north?utm_source=switchsg&amp;utm_medium=website&amp;utm_id=switch+on) has played an integral role at the centre of Singapore’s innovation and startup ecosystem. The R&amp;D hub is home to growth sectors including biomedical sciences, infocomm technology and media, as well as startups, with over 400 leading companies, 700 startups and 50,000 knowledge workers.

![](https://lh7-us.googleusercontent.com/bnKlrDjcmXk9dHFIttTZh96auy9XWgo6DDvTsLBa27ICwr86GX20tWAat0XuywqZEygAkHtH_Zgu_2n7hdaIHf-EPU7pegUeid3hCaGTnMQ3TfOpL0aiiCi9q9vk9Ham3Pe7djn7YY86zk5KNtWm85o)

To attend a lab crawl or innovation tour, you will need to be a SWITCH Trade Pass holder.

##### **About JTC Corporation**

![](https://lh7-us.googleusercontent.com/9t7tXnq4-b_LCPLsrfu0J8QcpH8DAH1o4d1Yev4US1R5Mx9RiJFTUAeMr298-NEeEeQxs4K3atfehxHA4wt0q07VE6dSp9z0mV-RpgiDk4zSR5wqWRSkxmbwDNUbSa7nu5sVanwOVUo8x4ggjnwekXA)
A government agency under Singapore’s Ministry of Trade and Industry, JTC is paving the way forward for Singapore’s industrial landscape by championing clean, green and smart estates. It provides infrastructure, systems and initiatives that bridge cutting-edge technology and human skill to fuel growth.
