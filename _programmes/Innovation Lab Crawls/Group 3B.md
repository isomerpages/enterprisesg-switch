#### Labs you will visit

#### MiRXES Lab

![SWITCH Innovation Lab Crawls at MiRXES](/images/2023/Lab%20Crawls/2023_switch_lab_crawls_mirxes.jpg)
<sup>Photo courtesy of [MiRXES](http://www.mirxes.com/)</sup>

Mirxes Lab is an ultra-high-throughput research laboratory in Singapore’s Biopolis. Mirxes’s early detection test kits are powered by our proprietary and patented miRNA detection technology platform. Through extensive research, we have an in-depth understanding of the unique biology of miRNA as disease biomarkers, particularly at early stages of the disease. We also integrate data from diverse genomic and epigenomic sources, to discover high performance and robust biomarkers for diagnostic applications. At Mirxes Lab, our dedicated researchers strive to unify the advancements in biomolecular research and data science, as well as conduct clinical studies for product development and validation to address unmet clinical needs.

Both Mirxes Lab and M Diagnostics, a state-of-the-art molecular diagnostic laboratory, will also provide the crucial infrastructures to the development, validation and implementation of the multi-cancer early detection (MCED) test under Project CADENCE.

**Mirxes Lab Crawl Details**
* Name of Lab: Mirxes Lab
* Lab Address: 11 Biopolis Way, #08-03/04/05 Helios, Singapore 138667

#### co11ab Novena
![SWITCH Innovation Lab Crawl at co11ab Novena](/images/2023/Lab%20Crawls/2023_switch_lab_crawls_co11abnovena.jpg)
<sup>_Photo courtesy of [co11ab](http://www.co11ab.sg/)_</sup>

co11ab Novena–Singapore’s First BioMedtech Incubator Within A Health City

co11ab is a BioMedtech incubator, a tripartite alliance between the Nanyang Technological University, Singapore (NTU), the Agency for Science, Technology, and Research (A\*STAR), the National Healthcare Group (NHG). Our mission is to be a national innovation and translational hub in HealthCity Novena by creating receptable to support medical technology translations, spinoffs creation and incubation.

**co11ab Lab Crawl Details**
* Name of Lab: co11ab Novena
* Lab Address: Lee Kong Chian School of Medicine, Clinical Sciences Building, 11 Mandalay Road #16-01, Singapore 308232
