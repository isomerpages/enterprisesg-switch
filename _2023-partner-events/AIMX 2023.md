#### Bringing the Global AI Ecosystem Together

AIMX or AI Maxima, provides a platform that aims to answer the biggest question around AI today and the future: HOW.

Converging global players from industry, government, academia, research and philanthropy, AIMX seeks to address real-world issues of AI Governance and AI for Good, while keeping the pulse on issues that impact AI for Industry. The inaugural edition of AIMX will comprise a 2-day conference and 3-day exhibition, to be hosted by the Singapore Week of Innovation &amp; Technology (SWITCH) 2023, from 31 October to 2 November 2023 at the Sands Expo &amp; Convention Centre, Singapore.

*AIMX is a partner event of SWITCH. All registrations to this event will also be able to access the [SWITCH Trade Floor](https://2023.switchsg.org/mvKw9?RefId=Trade) from 31 Oct–2 Nov 2023.*
