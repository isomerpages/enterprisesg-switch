#### Retail. Marketing. Commerce.

FUTR Asia unites the brightest minds in the fields of retail, marketing and commerce. Over 400 executives will attend the 2023 event, gathering to discuss the future consumer, the latest in retail and eCommerce strategy, marketing innovation and how AI will weave itself into every facet of the industries we operate in.

*FUTR Asia is a partner event of SWITCH. All registrations to this event will also be able to access the [SWITCH Trade Floor](https://2023.switchsg.org/mvKw9?RefId=Trade) from 31 Oct–2 Nov 2023.*
