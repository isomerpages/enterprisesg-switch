##### **Where Blockchain Meets Intellectual Property**

Why does Blockchain matter for Intellectual Property (IP)? How will blockchain impact the IP landscape? What is the future of blockchain and IP?&nbsp;

Join us to discuss the findings of a joint study by the Swiss Federal Institute of Intellectual Property (IPI) and Intellectual Property Office of Singapore (IPOS), to shed light on the intersection of blockchain and IP.

Learn how to boost blockchain adoption, how to strengthen the IP industry with blockchain, and how blockchain and IP can create transformative chain reactions with our Swiss and Singapore industry expert panel.&nbsp;

Whether you work with blockchains, dabble in IP, or are just curious about the topic, we invite you to be a part of our event.

<hr>

##### **About IPOS**

The Intellectual Property Office of Singapore (IPOS) is committed to developing Singapore into a global innovation hub. We help to catalyse a future driven by life-enhancing, commercially successful ideas created by businesses, professionals and everyday people. We help to turn today’s ideas into tomorrow’s assets.
