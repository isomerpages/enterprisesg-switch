#### Connect with startups, investors and corporates passionate about the Circular Economy

Co-organised by Enterprise Singapore, the National Environment Agency (NEA), and PUB, Singapore's National Water Agency, the Circular Economy Innovation Day aims to highlight the opportunities to close the loop in water and waste, both locally and in the region. This event is specially curated for startups, investors and corporates interested in sustainability and the circular economy.

Join us to hear from investors as they share about the latest trends and investments in circular economy technologies, with the added lens of impact investing. Corporates and stakeholders from both public and private sectors will also share about the opportunities for global startups to develop and deploy their solutions through open innovation and co-innovation partnerships.

*Circular Economy Innovation Day is a partner event of SWITCH. All registrations to this event will also be able to access the [SWITCH Trade Floor](https://2023.switchsg.org/mvKw9?RefId=Trade) from 31 Oct–2 Nov 2023.*
